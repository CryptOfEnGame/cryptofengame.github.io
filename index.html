Crypt of En

A world where magic and myth meets monsters and more! As you progress on your path to dominion, you must work to empower your team, summon your extraordinary creatures, and battle against the greatest of foes. Through the deepest dungeons to the abandoned fortresses, you will fight for the freedom of many and win the crown of Entland. Join us in the Crypt of En and game onwards toward victory!
